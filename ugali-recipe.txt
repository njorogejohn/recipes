recipe for ugali

ingredients
1. 2 cups of maize floor
2. 3 litres of water

procedure
1. boil the water in a caserole
2. add the flour into the water 
3. keep stirring till you make a paste
4. let it cook for 15 minutes, turning it periodically not to burn
5. Serve hot with stew of your choice
